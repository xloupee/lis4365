Image Format Comparison

PNG Format

PNG is best for:
- Images with transparency
- Screenshots
- Text-heavy images

GIF Format

GIF is best for:
- Animations
- Simple graphics
- Limited color images

JPG Format

JPG is best for:
- Photographs
- Complex images
- Web-optimized images
